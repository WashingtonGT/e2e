# The end-2-end intelligence automation platform

## Vision Statement

A general purpose platform to organize the world's knowledge and streamlize the human and machine collaboration.

## Capabilities

1. Define or manage the domain knowledge topology, and consolidate the semantic model (Global Knowledge)
2. Manage Enterprise Specifid domain knowledge graph
3. Manage goals, content and context
4. Manage the collaboration between human and machine.  (Workflow, communication etc.)
5. Manage the final knowledge product (knolwedge of solution, value), persistent, version control
